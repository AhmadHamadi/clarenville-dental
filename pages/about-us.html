Our Clinic

Dental care close to home, built around you

Clarenville Dental Care is a dental clinic located at 259 Memorial Drive, serving Clarenville and the surrounding communities of the Discovery Trail region. We offer a broad range of services — from routine cleanings and checkups to dental implants, Invisalign, emergency care, and more — all in one familiar, convenient location.

Our focus has always been on patient-first care: clear communication, honest recommendations, and an environment where you feel comfortable asking questions. We are currently accepting new patients and welcome families, individuals, and patients with CDCP or private insurance coverage.

  • Accepting new patients — no referral required
  • CDCP accepted & most private insurance plans welcome
  • Family, emergency, cosmetic, and restorative care
  • Open Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday 8am-5pm; Wednesday 8am-8pm; closed Saturday and Sunday

General Dentist

Dr. Reem Mamarbachi

  • Clear explanations before treatment begins
General Dentist

Dr. Anuprita Mehta

Dr. Anuprita Mehta is a general dentist at Clarenville Dental Care with a patient-focused approach to dental care. She brings calm, careful attention to appointments across preventive, general, and restorative dentistry.
