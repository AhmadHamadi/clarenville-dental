Smile Makeovers - Clarenville, NL

A smile makeover is about planning several cosmetic goals together instead of solving them one by one

Patients usually ask about a smile makeover when the concern is not just colour, or just spacing, or just one chipped tooth. It is usually about how the whole smile feels and looks together.

Planning First

The most important part of a smile makeover is usually deciding what should change first and what should stay conservative

A smile makeover often involves comparing more than one treatment path. Some patients may start with whitening. Others may be better served by Invisalign, bonding, veneers, or a mix of cosmetic and restorative work. The point is not to make treatment bigger than necessary. It is to build a plan where the pieces make sense together.

  • Looks at the smile as a whole instead of one issue in isolation
  • Often compares whitening, bonding, veneers, and alignment
  • Helps sequence treatment in a practical order
  • Useful when multiple visible concerns overlap

Setting Priorities

A good cosmetic plan balances appearance, tooth health, and what matters most to you

That might mean focusing on the teeth that show most when you smile. It might mean improving colour before shape. It might mean deciding that a small, natural-looking change is better than a dramatic one. The right smile makeover is not defined by how many procedures are included. It is defined by whether the plan actually fits the person.
