How to use your Canadian Dental Care Plan at a Clarenville dentist

The CDCP is a federal program that provides dental coverage to eligible Canadians without private insurance. If you are enrolled and need care in Clarenville, this article walks you through exactly how it works at the clinic — before you call.

CDCP Coverage Page Call (709) 200-0209

Who qualifies for the Canadian Dental Care Plan?

The CDCP is available to Canadian residents who do not have access to private dental insurance and whose adjusted family net income falls below a certain threshold. Eligibility is determined through the CRA, and most patients apply or confirm their status through Service Canada or Canada.ca.

If you are unsure whether you qualify, the quickest way to check is through the federal CDCP portal. The clinic cannot confirm your enrollment, but once you know you are enrolled, booking with your coverage is straightforward.

What does CDCP cover at a Clarenville dental clinic?

CDCP coverage includes a range of preventive and basic dental services, with partial coverage for some additional procedures depending on your income level and age group. Common covered services include:

  • Dental exams and X-rays
  • Teeth cleanings (dental hygiene appointments)
  • Fillings for cavities
  • Tooth extractions
  • Root canal treatment on some teeth
  • Dentures and some prosthetic work

Coverage percentages vary based on your income bracket and whether you are an adult, senior, or child under 18. Your co-payment — the portion you pay out-of-pocket — may range from 0% to 40% depending on these factors.

How to use your CDCP coverage at Clarenville Dental Care

Clarenville Dental Care is a CDCP-participating provider. This means you can use your coverage here without needing to seek out a specialist clinic or travel to another city.

When you book your appointment, let the team know you are a CDCP patient. The clinic will verify your coverage with the federal portal before your visit. Billing for your covered portion is handled directly — you pay only your co-payment (if applicable) at the time of your visit.

  • No paperwork to submit yourself — the clinic bills directly
  • Bring your provincial health card and confirm your CDCP enrollment
  • Your co-payment is due at the time of service
  • Coverage is renewed annually — check your status each year

What CDCP does not cover

CDCP does not cover cosmetic procedures such as teeth whitening, veneers, or Invisalign. It also does not cover dental implants. If you need a service that falls outside the covered scope, the clinic will let you know the cost before proceeding so there are no surprises.

Ready to book with your CDCP coverage?

If you are enrolled in the CDCP and need dental care in Clarenville, you do not need to look far. Call the clinic at (709) 200-0209 or use the booking form and mention that you have CDCP coverage. The team will confirm everything before your visit.
