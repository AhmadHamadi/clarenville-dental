Implant Crowns - Clarenville, NL

An implant crown is the visible replacement tooth attached to a single implant support

For patients replacing one missing tooth, an implant crown is often the part of treatment that restores appearance and chewing in a way that feels closest to a natural tooth.

Single-Tooth Replacement

The crown is the visible part of a completed single-tooth implant case

When one tooth is missing, an implant crown is often used to restore the space. The crown is designed to sit on the implant support and match the surrounding teeth in shape, colour, and function as closely as possible.

  • Used for many single missing-tooth cases
  • Designed to fit your bite and surrounding smile
  • Part of broader implant restoration planning
  • Focused on function as well as appearance

Comparing Approaches

An implant crown is one option among several ways to replace a single tooth

Some patients compare an implant crown with a conventional bridge or a removable option. The right fit depends on the condition of neighbouring teeth, the overall treatment plan, and what kind of restoration makes the most sense practically.
