How to Book

The fastest ways to schedule your visit

Calling is the quickest way to book — especially for urgent concerns, complex situations, or when you are not sure what type of appointment you need. The clinic can triage your concern and get you the right appointment type in one call.

  • Call (709) 200-0209 during clinic hours
  • Send a message via the contact page for non-urgent requests
  • Mention you are a new patient when you call so the right first-visit appointment can be scheduled
  • If you have insurance or CDCP coverage, mention it when booking so it can be verified in advance
  • If your concern is urgent, do not wait on a message reply — call directly
