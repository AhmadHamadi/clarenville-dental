Your First Visit

What to bring to your first appointment

Coming prepared makes your first visit smoother and helps the team understand your dental history from the start.

  • Photo ID and provincial health card
  • Insurance card or benefit plan details (if you have coverage)
  • List of any current medications
  • Notes on any dental concerns, pain, or questions
  • Previous dental records or X-rays if available (not required)

What Happens Next

What to expect when you come in

Most first visits follow a predictable flow so you know what you are walking into — no surprises.

  • A brief intake review of your health history and concerns
  • A full dental exam including a look at your teeth, gums, and bite
  • X-rays if clinically indicated and not recently taken
  • A cleaning if time permits and scheduled
  • A clear explanation of any findings and recommended next steps
