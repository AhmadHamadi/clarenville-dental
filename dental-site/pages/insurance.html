Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)

The Canadian Dental Care Plan is a federal government program providing dental coverage to eligible Canadians who don't have access to private dental insurance. Clarenville Dental Care is a registered CDCP provider.

The plan covers a range of dental services including examinations, X-rays, cleanings, fillings, extractions, and more depending on your eligibility level.

To check if you qualify, visit the Government of Canada website or contact our office â€” we're happy to help you navigate the process at no charge.
