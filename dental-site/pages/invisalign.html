Clear Aligner Treatment

How Invisalign straightens your teeth

Invisalign uses a series of custom-made clear plastic aligners to move teeth gradually into the correct position. Each aligner is worn for one to two weeks, then replaced with the next in the sequence.

Because the aligners are removable, you can eat normally and brush and floss without working around brackets or wires. Most patients wear them 20 to 22 hours per day for best results.

  • Custom aligners made from digital scans
  • Removable for eating and oral hygiene
  • Virtually invisible when worn
  • Treats crowding, spacing, and mild bite issues
  • Progress tracked at periodic check-in visits

Is Invisalign Right for You?

Who Invisalign works well for

Invisalign is effective for a wide range of alignment concerns in teens and adults, including crowding, spacing, overbite, underbite, and crossbite. Cases with significant skeletal misalignment may require a different approach.

A consultation is the only way to know for certain whether Invisalign fits your situation. We will take scans, review your bite, and walk you through what the treatment process would look like.

Straighter teeth are not just about appearance — alignment affects how you bite, chew, and clean your teeth.

Misaligned teeth are harder to clean thoroughly, which increases the risk of decay and gum disease over time. Invisalign fixes that problem discreetly, without the look or feel of traditional braces.
