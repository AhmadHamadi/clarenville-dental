Modern Dental Care

Technology that makes your visit better

Modern dental technology is not about complexity for its own sake — it is about finding problems more accurately, treating them more precisely, and making the experience more comfortable.

The equipment at Clarenville Dental Care is selected to improve clinical outcomes and patient experience. Less radiation, sharper images, more accurate impressions, and more predictable treatment results all come from having the right tools.

  • Digital X-rays with reduced radiation
  • Intraoral cameras for patient education
  • Digital impressions where applicable
  • Modern sterilisation and infection control
  • Comfortable, up-to-date treatment chairs

Why It Matters

Better technology means better diagnosis

Digital X-rays detect decay and bone changes that are not visible to the naked eye, often years before they become symptomatic. Catching a problem early consistently leads to simpler treatment and better outcomes.

Intraoral cameras allow patients to see what their dentist sees — which makes treatment recommendations easier to understand and decisions easier to make with confidence.
