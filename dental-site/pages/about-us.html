Our Clinic

Dental care close to home, built around you

Clarenville Dental Care is a full-service dental clinic located at 259 Memorial Drive, serving Clarenville and the surrounding communities of the Discovery Trail region. We offer a comprehensive range of services — from routine cleanings and checkups to dental implants, Invisalign, emergency care, and more — all in one familiar, convenient location.

Our focus has always been on patient-first care: clear communication, honest recommendations, and an environment where you feel comfortable asking questions. We are currently accepting new patients and welcome families, individuals, and patients with CDCP or private insurance coverage.

  • Accepting new patients — no referral required
  • CDCP accepted & most private insurance plans welcome
  • Family, emergency, cosmetic, and restorative care
  • Open Monday–Friday 8am–6pm and Saturday 9am–3pm
Our Dentists

Meet our doctors

Our dentists bring experience, care, and a genuine commitment to every patient they see.

General Dentist

Dr. Reem Mamarbachi, DDS

Dr. Reem Mamarbachi is the lead dentist at Clarenville Dental Care and brings warmth, precision, and genuine patient care to every appointment. With a focus on aesthetic dentistry, she helps patients achieve both healthy and beautiful smiles — whether through everyday preventive care or more involved cosmetic and restorative work.

Dr. Mamarbachi completed her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree and has continued building her expertise in areas including Invisalign, teeth whitening, composite bonding, and comprehensive treatment planning. She is known for taking time to explain what she finds and what options are available — clearly and without pressure.

  • Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS)
  • Focus: aesthetic and preventive dentistry
  • Experienced in Invisalign, cosmetic bonding, and implant restoration
  • Fluent in English and Arabic
General Dentist

Dr. Anuprita Mehta, DDS

Dr. Anuprita Mehta is a general dentist at Clarenville Dental Care with a patient-focused approach to all aspects of dental care. She brings calm, careful attention to each appointment and is skilled across the full spectrum of general and restorative dentistry — from fillings and extractions to crowns, root canals, and preventive care.

Dr. Mehta values building long-term relationships with patients and their families. She takes time to listen, explain treatment clearly, and ensure every patient feels confident and comfortable before any procedure begins.

  • Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS)
  • General, restorative, and preventive dentistry
  • Experienced with patients of all ages including children
